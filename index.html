I build rockets, spacecraft simulators, drones, and software.


My name is Alex Cameron Bailey and I'm an Aerospace Engineer and computer scientist with a degree from San Diego State University. I have wide variety of skills acquired over time through my roles in various engineering clubs such as our school liquid rocket team, Rocket Project, and Mechatronics. During my time with the rocket project I managed the Avionics section where we designed and built test stands for our engines and the various electrical systems involved with the rockets and ground stations. I was a part of the elctrical team with Mechatronics where we designed and built PCB's for an autonmous underwater sub. I also held leadership positions in our schools SPACE lab where we built spacecraft simulators for control algorithms. As the lead engineer I oversaw a DOD research project in order to give the Marine Corps new drone capabilities. I've obtained internships at major defense contractors such as General Atomics Aeronautical systems, Northrop Grumman, and even Nasa's Jet propulsion labratory.
